The Legend

Sidhu Moosewala was an influential Punjabi singer, rapper, and songwriter known for blending traditional Punjabi music with modern rap. He gained massive popularity for his bold lyrics and unique style.Beyond his music, he also ventured into politics. Tragically, Moosewala was shot dead in May 2022, leaving behind a lasting legacy in both Punjabi culture and global music scenes.
